Welcome to CAFE RISIKO


For over 35 years, we have been the only restaurant in Copenhagen offering a unique blend of intercontinental cuisines that cater to every palate. Step into our cozy and warm atmosphere, where every visit feels like coming home. Whether you're here for a casual meal or to celebrate a special occasion, our fine dining experience ensures unforgettable moments. Join us and indulge in a culinary journey that combines tradition, flavor, and the comfort of a home-like environment.

Pasta Tartuffo

This picture showcases a luxurious pasta dish featuring tender okse tenderloin slices, perfectly cooked and resting atop a bed of pasta. The pasta is generously coated in a rich, earthy truffle sauce, adding a deep, aromatic flavor. Complementing the truffle sauce is a creamy mushroom sauce, blending seamlessly to create a harmonious and indulgent taste. Every element—succulent meat, velvety sauces, and al dente pasta—comes together to create a gourmet experience that is both visually stunning and irresistibly delicious.
