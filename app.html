Welcome to CAFE RISIKO


For over 35 years, we have been the only restaurant in Copenhagen offering a unique blend of intercontinental cuisines that cater to every palate. Step into our cozy and warm atmosphere, where every visit feels like coming home. Whether you're here for a casual meal or to celebrate a special occasion, our fine dining experience ensures unforgettable moments. Join us and indulge in a culinary journey that combines tradition, flavor, and the comfort of a home-like environment.

Pasta Al Tartuffo

This picture showcases a luxurious pasta dish featuring tender okse tenderloin slices, perfectly cooked and resting atop a bed of pasta. The pasta is generously coated in a rich, earthy truffle sauce, adding a deep, aromatic flavor. Complementing the truffle sauce is a creamy mushroom sauce, blending seamlessly to create a harmonious and indulgent taste. The dish is garnished with fresh herbs, adding a pop of color and freshness. Every element—succulent meat, velvety sauces, and al dente pasta—comes together to create a gourmet experience that is both visually stunning and irresistibly delicious.

Artisanal Mixology Experience

Step into a world of mixology magic where every cocktail tells a story. Our master bartenders craft liquid artistry using premium spirits, fresh ingredients, and boundless creativity. Watch as timeless techniques meet innovative flair, transforming classic recipes into modern masterpieces. Savor the dance of flavors in every carefully measured pour, shaken to perfection, and garnished with precision.
